Invisible Obstacles: The Struggles of International Students in STEM Careers

Intro: International students in the U.S. face significant barriers in pursuing STEM careers, from restrictive visa policies to cultural hurdles and limited support networks. These challenges not only affect their opportunities but also deprive the American workforce of valuable talent and diverse perspectives. This essay explores strategies for addressing these challenges and highlights the benefits of integrating more international students into the U.S. job market.

These systemic challenges not only limit the aspirations of talented international students but also deprive the American workforce of innovative minds that could drive economic growth and enrich industries with diverse perspectives and skills.
